# Brainstorming & Design

## Phases
1. **Problem Definition**: What problem are we solving? What are the constraints?
2. **Solution Exploration**: List 3+ potential approaches. Don't judge yet.
3. **Trade-off Analysis**: Compare approaches based on complexity, performance, and maintainability.
4. **Selection**: Choose the best path and justify it.
5. **High-Level Design**: Sketch the architecture and components.

## Output
A clear design document or "Plan Mode" proposal.
